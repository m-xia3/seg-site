Certifications · Compliance

We rely on third-party standards—not slogans. Our program covers social responsibility, quality systems and recycled material chain-of-custody.

Higg FSLM · Verified

Facility Social & Labor Module implemented and third-party verified (Intertek). Scope includes wages & working hours, health & safety, management systems and employee welfare. Results can be checked on the Worldly/Higg platform by factory name.

ISO 9001:2015

Quality Management System covering the design and production of vulcanized and cold-cement footwear. Uses a process approach and risk-based thinking with documented operations, supplier evaluation, CAPA, and customer-satisfaction monitoring to ensure consistent delivery.

RCS Scope (Footwear)

Recycled Claim Standard scope certificate (Bureau Veritas). Supports material traceability and chain-of-custody documentation; certified nodes issue documents that brands can verify.

Note: Certificate scans and platform links are available on request as part of our compliance pack. Other programs (e.g., Higg FEM, BSCI, GRS) can be advanced with brand projects; we do not claim them until completed.
