Milestones

From a 1994 start to a 2015 integrated model—evolving into today’s multi-origin, single-standard platform.

Qingzhou Baozu Footwear Co., Ltd. was founded in 2001 as our first factory. In 2015, Handan Baozu Footwear Co., Ltd. was established as our second factory and—together with Hong Kong Tai Yuk Trading Limited—forms our integrated trade-and-manufacturing model. Today the Handan campus covers 65,000㎡ of land with 45,000㎡ of workshops; both campuses operate within one coordinated system.
